Our Goals

Environmental impact

Food waste contributes to greenhouse gas emissions and other negative environmental impacts. By reducing food waste, we can help mitigate climate change and other environmental problems.

Hunger and food insecurity

Millions of people around the world go hungry every day, while large amounts of food are being wasted. An app that helps reduce food waste can also help provide food to those in need.

Economic benefits

Reducing food waste can help save money for individuals, businesses, and governments. For example, restaurants can save money on food costs, and governments can reduce the cost of waste disposal.

Ethical concerns

Throwing away food when people are hungry is morally questionable. An app against food waste can help raise awareness of the issue and encourage people to take action.

Health benefits

Food that is thrown away is often still edible, but it may not be as fresh as it once was. By reducing food waste, we can help ensure that people are eating fresher, healthier food.

Social responsibility

As a society, we have a responsibility to reduce waste and protect the planet. An app against food waste can help individuals and businesses take steps towards achieving this goal.
